Data Visualization Assignment

Hollywood Movie Dataset 2007-2011

Having this dataset could help us to look through the movies with the highest gross rate in the period of 2007-2011. The following visualizations could help us to understand poeple's preferences and find the most succesfull studios in Hollywood. The questons that I am going to answer are as follow:

  • What are the most succesfull movies?
  • What are the most succesfull studios and their movies?
  • What are the highest rated studios by the audiance?

World's movies with highest Gross

First we will identify the movies that earned most money. Below we can see the graph that Twilight is on the first place, from which we can assume that due to an already released book, poeple had much more interest. Advertisements could play an important role as well. Another assumption that we can make is that gross more or less presents the demand of people to watch certain movie. As more poeple visit it the gross will increase.

Top Movie Studios and their movies with a total Gross

In the graph below we can see the top movie studios and the movies they released. An interesting outlier is the 'Independent' studio, by looking at its movies we can assume that its Twilight movie brought it to third place, while the rest films are lower on average compared to the other studios. Same we could assume for the 'Summit' studio which had its gross incereased thanks to the Twilight movies. More or less we can conclude that Twilight turned into franchise and helped both studios.

Top Studios sorted by audiance score

Now after knowing the top studios by their gross, let's check the average audiance score of the studios to see which comes in top place.

Top Movies sorted by audiance score

In the below graph we see the top movies that received the highest audiance score along with their studios. We can say that WALL-E satisfied most of its clients. While the other movies had a huge demand by the visitors, they tended to score less rating, where some movies are not even in the top for audiance score. As a conclusion we can say that Disney is one of the most succesfull and satisfied movie studios for the period 2007-2011.
